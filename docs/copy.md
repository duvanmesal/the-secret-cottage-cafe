## Section 4 — Story

**Pull quote (h2, Playfair Display italic):**
> "We didn't want to open a café. We wanted to create a place where time slows down."

**Body paragraph 1:**
> The Secret Cottage Café began as a weekend ritual — two friends, a cast-iron kettle, and a kitchen that smelled of cardamom and warm bread. What started as a private morning habit became something we felt we had to share.

**Body paragraph 2:**
> We found the cottage in 2018. A year of slow renovation later, we opened with six tables, a wood-burning stove, and the belief that the best coffee is the one you drink without rushing.

**Body paragraph 3:**
> Everything here is made by hand. The jams, the pastries, the candles, the playlists. Even the cups come from a ceramicist two villages away.

**Caveat accent:**
> *Everything made here, with time.*
