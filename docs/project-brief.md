# Project Brief — The Secret Cottage Café

## Overview

**Project name:** The Secret Cottage Café  
**Type:** Premium scroll-driven 3D landing page  
**Stack:** Astro, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, GSAP + ScrollTrigger, Lenis, Spline, React Three Fiber, Motion, Zustand

---

## Concept

The user doesn't visit a website. They enter a place.

As the page loads, the user finds themselves at the edge of a forest path leading to a hidden cottage café. Scrolling becomes walking. Each section of the page is a new room, a new moment, a new sensory layer — the crunch of gravel underfoot, morning light through linen curtains, the smell of fresh coffee implied through texture and motion.

The experience ends at a reservation — an invitation to continue the story in real life.

---

## Goal

Make the café feel like an immersive, cinematic experience rather than a conventional business website.

The visitor should leave feeling that The Secret Cottage Café is a place they *need* to visit — not because they were told to, but because they lived a small version of it on screen.

---

## Target Audience

- Adults 25–45 who value slow mornings, quality coffee, and aesthetic experiences
- Design-conscious travelers and locals seeking hidden-gem venues
- Food and lifestyle content creators
- People who choose a café based on feeling, not just ratings

## Visual References

- English countryside cottage cafés
- Handmade ceramic cups and artisan pastries
- Warm wood surfaces, linen napkins, dried flowers
- Garden mornings with soft golden-hour light
- Coffee steam rising in quiet stillness
- Forest paths with dappled sunlight

---

## Success Criteria

1. The landing page communicates premium quality without a single word about "quality"
2. The scroll journey feels continuous and cinematic, not page-by-page
3. The 3D coffee viewer creates a genuine moment of delight
4. Reservation CTA feels like an invitation, not a sales push
5. Build time stays under 10 seconds; no runtime errors

---

## Constraints

- No new libraries beyond the current stack
- Astro components for static/structural sections
- React islands only for interactive or animated areas
- GSAP ScrollTrigger owns scroll-based animation
- Motion handles UI microinteractions only
- Spline owns the main 3D scenes
- React Three Fiber for custom particle/steam/petal effects only
